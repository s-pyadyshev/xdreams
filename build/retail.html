Where finding “the one”

In store is where customers meet our Bed Experts and leave one step closer to their perfect night’s sleep.

A bed is never just a bed

Which is why we’ve got over 200 stores nationwide, filled with trained Bed Experts ready to help the Great British public find their perfect match.

In-store, no two days are the same. One minute you’ll be listening closely to a customer’s needs, the next you’ll be guiding them through high-value purchases with confidence and care. With our Sleepmatch technology on hand, you’ll turn what could be a confusing decision into an exciting (and reassuring) one.

We make sure every in-store visit is exceptional, and that starts with equipping you with the right tools, training and support to do your best work. Because a great night’s sleep is personal — and so is the service we give.

Over 200 stores nationwide

At Dreams, our retail stores are much more than showrooms—they’re where we turn the art of restful sleep into a joyful, friendly experience. Our network of stores truly stretches from Aberdeen to Truro.

Our regional stores are dynamic hubs that welcome a diverse range of roles, From Retail Sales Assistants who guide customers to their dream bed using our unique Sleepmatch technology, creating a memorable and personalised experience. To our Retail Store Managers who lead their in store team, deliver exceptional customer experiences.
