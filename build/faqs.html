FAQs

Because no one should lose sleep over job applications

Got questions?

We’ve got answers.

Think of this as your bedside guide to the application process – short, helpful and with a smile along the way.

How do I apply for
a role?

Ready to dream big with us?

Just follow a few simple steps to set up your account and upload your CV. Easy as fluffing a pillow.

Can I apply
for more than one
position at a time?

Absolutely – you can apply for more than one role

Just not the same one twice (we know you’re keen, but one dream at a time).

What happens
after I submit my
application?

You’ll get an email confirming we’ve received it.

Then we’ll review it and if it’s a dream match, a hiring manager will be in touch.

How long does the
recruitment process
take?

We try to respond as quickly as possible, but it may take up to 21 days.

Hang tight – we’re working through the pile like a stack of cosy duvets.

Have a
question for us?

We’re here to help
